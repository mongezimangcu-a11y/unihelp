# When do NSFAS applications open and close?

One of the most common questions students ask every year is: **when do NSFAS applications open and close?**

The answer changes from year to year, which is why students should always check the official NSFAS announcements. However, there is still a general pattern that can help you prepare early and avoid missing important deadlines.

## NSFAS application dates change each year

NSFAS does **not always open and close on the exact same dates** every year.

Usually, NSFAS announces:
- the opening date for new applications
- the closing date
- appeal windows
- document requirements
- updates about late submissions or system issues

Because of this, students should never assume the previous year’s closing date will be the same again.

## When do applications usually open?

NSFAS applications often open **towards the second half of the year** for students who want funding for the following academic year.

This is why it is important to start preparing early, even before the exact application date is announced.

## When do applications usually close?

Applications usually close a few weeks or months after they open, depending on the cycle.

This means students should not wait until the last minute. If the website is busy, your documents are missing, or your details are incorrect, you may run out of time.

## Why students miss the NSFAS deadline

Many students miss the application window because they:

- wait too long to apply
- do not prepare documents early
- use the wrong email or phone number
- have trouble uploading documents
- assume they can apply after the deadline
- do not follow official updates

Missing the deadline can delay your funding plans and create unnecessary stress.

## What should you prepare before NSFAS opens?

Even before the official date is announced, you can prepare:

- your South African ID or birth certificate
- your parent or guardian details if needed
- proof of income where required
- supporting documents
- a working phone number
- an email address you can access easily

Having these ready makes the application process much easier.

## Where should you check the dates?

Always rely on the **official NSFAS website and official NSFAS channels** for confirmed dates.

Do not trust random social media posts, forwarded WhatsApp messages, or unofficial websites claiming to know the dates before NSFAS confirms them.

## What if you miss the closing date?

If you miss the application closing date, you may not be able to apply for that cycle.

In some cases, NSFAS may announce updates, extensions, or appeals for certain situations, but students should never depend on that.

The safest approach is always:
- apply early
- check your status
- keep copies of your documents
- monitor official updates

## Final advice

NSFAS application dates change every year, so students should treat each cycle as new and confirm the dates officially.

The best thing you can do is:
- prepare your documents early
- create your account in time
- apply as soon as applications open
- avoid waiting for the final day
