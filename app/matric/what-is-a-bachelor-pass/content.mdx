# What is a Bachelor pass in South Africa?

A **Bachelor pass** (sometimes called a *Bachelor’s Degree pass*) is a Matric pass level
that can allow you to apply for a **Bachelor’s degree** at a university — but it does not
guarantee admission. Universities still look at your **APS score**, subject requirements,
and available space.

---

## Minimum requirements for a Bachelor pass

To get a Bachelor pass, you generally need:

- **Home Language**: 40% or more
- **Four other subjects** (excluding Life Orientation): 50% or more
- **Two other subjects**: 30% or more  
- You must pass **at least 6 of your 7 subjects**

---

## What can you study with a Bachelor pass?

A Bachelor pass can allow you to apply for:

- BA degrees (Humanities / Social Sciences)
- BCom degrees (Commerce) *(often needs Maths)*
- BSc degrees (Science) *(usually needs Maths and sometimes Science subjects)*
- Education degrees (BEd) *(requirements depend on phase/major)*

Some degrees are highly competitive, so you may need a high APS and strong marks.
